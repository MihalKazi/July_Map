Timeline of Events

June 2024

Quota System Reinstated

Bangladesh Supreme Court reinstated the controversial quota system in government jobs, triggering widespread student protests across the country.

July 16, 2024

First Martyr - Abu Sayed

Abu Sayed from Rangpur became the first confirmed martyr of the movement, sparking nationwide outrage and intensifying protests.

July 16-20, 2024

Escalation of Violence

Protests spread across all major cities. Government forces, Chhatra League, and law enforcement agencies began violent crackdowns on peaceful protesters.

July 21-25, 2024

Peak of Unrest

The most violent phase of the movement with the highest number of casualties. Students continued to demand justice despite facing severe repression.

August 1-5, 2024

Final Phase

The movement reached its conclusion with significant political changes. The sacrifices of our martyrs were not in vain.

October 2024

Memorial Foundation Established

July Martyrs Memorial Foundation was established with hotline (16000) to support families and document the history of this movement.
